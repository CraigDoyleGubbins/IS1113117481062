Scouts

USA, Summer, 2010

During the summer of 2010, I went on a two week camping trip to the USA with my local scout troop. Around 25 of us stayed in Camp John H. Ware 3rd in Pennsylvania. The camp was equipped with a shop, a dining hall, a swimming pool, a shooting range and more.

We also went to Baltimore, visiting the national aquarium and other local attractions around the harbour. At the end of our camp, we went to New York City and stayed in a hostel for the last two days.

Since my visit to the USA, I've also been to Austria, London and the Isle of Man with the scouts. These experiences have made me much more interested in travelling the rest of the world.

Ski Trip

Italy, February, 2012

My 2nd year class in secondary school went on a week-long ski trip in Italy in 2013. As there were around 100 of us, we were split into smaller groups according to skill.

Around the ski resort were many Italian shops selling clothes and food such as pizza. Everyone's skills improved over the course of the trip.
